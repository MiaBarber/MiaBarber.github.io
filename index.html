Does Surgery Cause The Spread Of Cancer?

Summary of Project

I decided to take on a myth; Does Surgery Really Cause The Spread of Cancer? During my research I discovered that it is possible for your cancer to spread during surgery, but if you have a good enough doctor most likely it will not happen.

Another way to discover cancer spread was the size of the biospy needle used on your tumor by your doctor. If your needle is small, because of other surrounding cancer cells, your cancer is most likely to spread than with a bigger needle. Knowing these facts has allowed me to give insight with some of my friends who are also cancer patients.
